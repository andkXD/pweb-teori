Overview

A UI/UX case study exploring how information architecture and visual hierarchy can improve the usability of a digital product. The project involved user research, wireframing, and high-fidelity prototyping in Figma.

Key focus areas included accessibility considerations, component-based design systems, and iterative testing. The final prototype demonstrated a measurable improvement in task completion rate compared to the baseline interface.
