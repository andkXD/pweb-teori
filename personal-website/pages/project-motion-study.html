Overview

A series of motion design explorations studying how timing, easing, and rhythm affect the feeling of digital interfaces. Combining After Effects comps with web-based GSAP implementations.

Each study isolates a single principle — anticipation, follow-through, squash & stretch — and translates it from classical animation theory into modern CSS and JavaScript motion.
